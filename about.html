ABOUT US

At ManZel Financial, we're committed to providing you convenience and simplicity in handling all of your business and financial needs—effortlessly and efficiently. As an entrepreneur, you need time and energy to grow your business, but that time and energy is all too often wasted on critical but mundane tasks. Our goal is to help you free up that space, so you can use it on creativity, innovation, and actively succeeding in your goals.

The primary goal of ManZel Financial is your financial empowerment. We bring a wealth of knowledge and know-how to our OneStop FinTech Shop Setting, delivering the help you need in the most convenient way possible to ensure you have enhanced control and compliance for your organization with services provided by ManZel and/or our trusted third-party partners.

At ManZel Financial, we're as committed to innovation as you are. Our Business Process Optimization team has crafted an impressive range of smart process-based tools, useful assessments, benchmarking strategies and other ways to help grow and propel your business. We couple our expertise with your organization's unique culture, timeline, and business requirements to achieve optimal results.

OUR PROCESS

How does it all work? What can you expect? If you like, we can start from the beginning—with a comprehensive documentation of your current processes. We'll assess upstream implications to identify and help you prioritize inefficiencies and barriers to success. We look for both redundancies and opportunities to streamline operations enabling revenue assurance, and recommend process improvement plans based on best practices and industry benchmarks. We leverage these insights to help develop a highly detailed, personalized implementation roadmap to ensure the timely achievement of your key objectives.

Clearing the way to innovation

In our experience, one of the highest barriers to success in a new business is the overload of small but important tasks necessary in the business landscape. It's easy to get overwhelmed.

With ManZel Financial, you can put those business needs on our plate, where we can handle them efficiently, expertly and—always— to your complete satisfaction. This gives you back the time you need to focus on the creativity and innovation your business requires.

Clearing the way to capital

For entrepreneurs who are otherwise engaged in growing their company, finding capital for funding can be fraught. We're all used to finding the information we require online, but online scams and "bad actors" make finding access to the right amount of capital difficult.

At ManZel Financial, we offer a safe way to explore your options. We offer free information, updated on our secure client portal, to provide you with many options on how you can obtain these funds. You may also choose a consultation, where one of our expert financial advisors can assist you in discovering how to build tailored, diversified financial portfolios.

We are here to help

We know what it takes to succeed in business. Whether you're just starting out, looking for your next win, or stuck on a problem somewhere in the middle—we can help.

With a combination of expert financial counselors, business consults and state-of-the-art algorithms, we match you with the right sequences of opportunities to gain the financial empowerment you need to grow your business and wealth portfolios.

Our expertise includes managing (and exceeding) consumer expectations; finding emerging technologies to reduce disruption risks; optimizing company culture and internal processes, and so much more. We can scale our services to support changes that range from small tactical improvements to large-scale transformations.

Our business consultants are experienced leaders and practitioners who are customer focused, driven by excellence, and dedicated to your success—however you define it and whatever you want it to be. No matter where you are in the journey, we can help you get there.
